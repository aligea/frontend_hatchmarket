LIFESPAN
MONSTERS' AVERAGE LENGTH OF LIFE. YOU CAN ONLY CONTROL AND INTERACT WITH YOUR MONSTERS WHEN THEY ARE ACTIVE AND ALIVE. PLEASE TAKE CARE OF YOUR MONSTERS BY FEEDING THEM ITEMS FOUND IN THE SHOP.
TARGET
A COLLECTION OF ITEMS TO BE TACKLED IN ORDER FOR YOU TO EARN COINS.THERE ARE 3 TYPES OF TARGET: WOOD, STONE AND GOLD. EACH OF THESE TARGET PRODUCE DIFFERENT AMOUNT OF COINS AT DIFFERENT REGIONS.
ABILITY
EVERY MONSTERS HAVE DIFFERENT ABILITIES. THEY ARE: PLANTING, LOGGING, EXPLORING, AND MINING. PLANTING AND LOGGING : IS ONLY FOR MONSTERS WHOSE TARGET IS WOOD, WHILE EXPLORING AND MINING : IS ONLY FOR THOSE WHOSE TARGETS ARE STONE OR GOLD. OF COURSE, THERE ARE RARE FEW THAT HAVE SEVERAL ABILITIES AND TARGETS.
SPEED
THE PACE OF THE MONSTERS' ACTION. THERE ARE 3 RANGE OF SPEED : SLOW (4 HOURS), MEDIUM (2 HOURS) AND RAPID (1 HOUR).
SPECIALITY
Speciality monsters have different specialities, some will earned you bonus items while others coins or diamonds. Increase your monsters' lifespan to get the extra benefit gained only after certain number of eggs has been hatched by other hatchers
